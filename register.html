Registration



The conference registration includes access to all workshops, tutorials, all conference sessions, lunches on all conference days, access to the banquet dinner, and one electronic copy of the proceedings.


For each accepted paper, at least one author must register at Authors Rate on or before 31 October 2016. Without the registration, the paper will not be included in the ACM digital library.


A few fellowships to cover registrations either completely or partially for authors from India, students from India, and academics/R&D Institutions from India are available. Such people may write to the Organizing Chair in advance.


Only students enrolled in full time programmes are eligible for registering at the student rate. Interested students should send a letter to the Organizing Chair stating their enrolment in their university signed by Dean/ Head of Department. The students rate is not applicable for students who are authors of accepted papers.
