Call for Papers


The world of computing has witnessed tremendous growth in the last two decades. There has been significant scientific and technical growth in the areas of computing as well as informatics. Due to advancements in computing hardware as well as significant development in the areas of artificial intelligence and machine learning, it has become possible to process huge volume of data at ease. Such developments have impacted almost all areas of technology.

This international conference is aimed at covering all major areas of computing as well as informatics. The conference shall provide a platform to share the latest research results in different dimensions of computing and informatics. Moreover, the conference will facilitate students, faculty members, researchers, engineers, practitioners, and interested persons in converging into one platform, where exchange of recent research results and brilliant ideas will take place.


Various tracks/ topics of the conference are as mentioned below (but not limited to):
  • Artificial Intelligence & Expert Systems
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Communication Networks
  • Cloud Technologies
  • Internet of Things
  • Data Science & Data Analytics
  • Healthcare Informatics
  • Cryptography
  • Information Security & Network Security
  • Cyber Security
  • Data Mining
  • Information Retrieval
  • Image Processing & Pattern Recognition
  • Quantum Computing & Communication
  • Social Networks


ICETC 2022 will comprise a highly selective technical program consisting of refereed submitted papers, panel discussions and focused workshops on emerging topics of Distributed Computing and Networking. ICETC 2022 will be held in Hyderabad, India during September 1-2, 2022. Conference papers will be published in the ACM Digital Library. Selected papers will be invited for fast-track publication in high-quality journals.
