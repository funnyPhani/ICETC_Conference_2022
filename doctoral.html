Call for Doctoral Symposium


ICETC 2022 will host a Doctoral Symposium, to encourage research scholars to share their research proposal, practices and methods, and to promote networking and collaboration with peers in their fields. The Symposium is an opportunity for students to obtain helpful feedback of their research from leading academics and practitioners from industries.


PhD students working in any area addressed by one or more topics mentioned below can submit a write-up describing their research proposal.


  • Distributed Systems: design, analysis, and complexity
  • Embedded systems, Multiprocessor and multi-core architectures
  • Ad-hoc, sensor and mesh networks
  • Data center and cloud networking
  • Formal Methods for Distributed Computing and Network Assurance
  • Distributed Computing Platforms for Open Stack Cloud
  • Software Defined Networks and Network Function Virtualization
  • Mobile and pervasive computing
  • Energy-efficient networking & Smart Grid
  • Traffic modeling and traffic routing
  • Authentication, security, privacy, trust in networks
  • Internet of Things
  • Multiplex and interdependent networks
  • Network robustness, Fault tolerance
  • Delay tolerant networks
  • Dynamic and time-varying networks
  • Cellular networks (4G/LTE)
  • Social networking and big data analytics
  • Overlay and P2P Networks and Services
  • Epidemics, Information diffusion and knowledge transfer in networks

Event Details

Doctoral Symposium will be an event consisting of short oral presentations (10 minutes) followed by poster presentations.Undisclosed judges will evaluate all presenters based on their oral and poster presentation for the Best Presentation Award. We plan to have the selected proceedings published through ACM and have it available in ACM Digital library.


Submission Guidelines

ICETC 2022 solicits high quality original manuscripts. Participants should submit the following

1. Research Proposal (a 5-page write-up) in standard ACM format
2. Curriculum Vitae
3. Advisor's Recommendation Letter

The research proposal must include the clear problem statement of the research with motivation (preferably with examples), scientific and technical approach, and the results obtained so far. Sufficient information should be provided for the chairs and the review board to judge the merit of the research. The submission should highlight the name of the author.


Participants should upload the Research proposal, CV and advisor's recommendation letter in a Single pdf file. Moreover, already published contents are allowed to be included in the proposal, with corresponding references.

Eligibility

Though this symposium is primarily targeted towards PhD students, exceptional BS and MS students who have formulated a research project and obtained some good results will also be considered and hence are encouraged to apply. A student is defined as anyone who is a full time student at the time of submission in any university or institute.


The submission link

All submissions must be made electronically in printable pdf form via the EasyChair submission management system. Once logged in, kindly select "Doctoral Symposium" from the list of tracks, and then click on "Continue", to access the submission page for the Doctoral Symposium.
