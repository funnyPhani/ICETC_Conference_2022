Workshops


The 18th International Conference on Distributed Computing and Networking ( ICETC 2022 ) is hosting 3 technical workshops collocated with the conference. Papers describing original research work and practical experiences/experimental results are solicitated on topics related to the following workshops in the distributed computing and networking areas. The workshop titles include.


→ The 6th International Workshop on Computing and Networking for Internet of Things (ComNet-IoT 2017)


→ 1st International Workshop on Algorithms & Architectures for Distributed Data Analytics (AADDA)


→ 1st International Workshop on Distributed Platforms and Technologies for Financial Services (DiPTeFS)


Technical paper submissions to the workshops are due on October 21st, 2016. Accepted workshop technical papers will be indexed by the ACM Digital Library. Researchers have the additional option to submit work-in-progress papers that will not be published by the ACM, but that would be welcome for presentation at the workshop. Such work-in-progress papers will also undergo a rigorous review, and the authors are requested to clearly indicate that their submission is of such a nature on the cover page for the submitted manuscript. All paper submissions should not exceed 6 pages.

The details regarding each of the workshops ( workshop websites, cfp's, submission links, topics of interest ) are available in the call for papers for each workshop links given above. Please do review the information provided in the workshop websites, and please do submit your latest research to these workshops if applicable. At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present the paper. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to write to either of the workshop chairs Dilip Krishnaswamy ( IBM Research, dilip@ieee.org ) or Professor Sathya Peri ( IIT Hyderabad, peri.sathya@gmail.com ). We look forward to your submissions.

Sincerely,

The ICETC 2022 Conference and Workshop Chairs.and Conference Committee
