First International Conference on Emerging Trends in Computing and Informatics (ICETC 2022)


ICETC (First International Conference on Emerging Trends in Computing and Informatics ) is a premier international conference dedicated to addressing advances in Distributed Computing and Communication Networks, which over the years, has become a leading forum for disseminating the latest research results in these fields. The 18th edition of this conference will be organised in Mangalagiri, hosted by SRM-AP University.



ICETC 2022 will comprise a highly selective technical program consisting of refereed submitted papers, panel discussions and focused workshops on emerging topics of Distributed Computing and Networking. ICETC 2022 will be held in Hyderabad, India during September 1-2, 2022. Conference papers will be published in the ACM Digital Library. Selected papers will be invited for fast-track publication in high-quality journals.
