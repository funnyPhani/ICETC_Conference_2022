Program Details:

During the conference days there will be


  • Research paper presentations in different tracks

  • Tutorials in different relevant and latest topics (3-4) (this is a pre-conference event on 31-May-2023)

  • Invited Speeches (3-5)

  • Keynote Addresses by Eminent Speakers (2-3)

  • Cultural Program & Conference Dinner

  • Conference Tour in nearby areas (depending on the interests of the participants)


Outcome of the Conference:


  • The presented papers will be published through a reputed international publisher in book format. The book will be sent for indexing by reputed indexing services such as Scopus.
