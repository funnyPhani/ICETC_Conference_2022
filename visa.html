General VISA Letter Instructions


Attendees are responsible for obtaining the appropriate travel authorization and visa for India, if needed. Information on visa requirements for entry into India are available at: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/.

Registered attendees who require a supporting letter from the conference for their visa application can fill the missing information in the two templates given below and send to icdcn2017@idrbt.ac.in and grgangadharan@idrbt.ac.in. We will email you the signed and scanned copy of these letters.
