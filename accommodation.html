ICETC 2022 Campus Accomodation


A limited number of air-conditioned single rooms (individual use only) are available, ONLY FOR CONFERENCE PARTICIPANTS, and will be offered on a first come first served basis. Campus accommodation does not permit participants to stay with family members. Tariff per Night is Rs. 1800/- (including breakfast, dinner, and lodging). Free Wi-Fi facility is available.

Participants are requested to book and pay in advance for IDRBT campus accommodation clearly specifying the check-in date and the check-out date.

Campus accommodation charges can be made through net banking facilities (NEFT/RTGS) with the following details:

About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the emerging knowledge hub of the nation, offers a fascinating blend of tradition and modernity. A strong Industrial, Commercial and Information Technology Center, Hyderabad is the only city in Southern India, where the major language spoken is Urdu. At the heart of the hustle-bustle of the city is its symbol, Charminar, the impressive square gateway with four minarets. Near the Charminar is the Mecca Masjid, one of the largest in the world. Another of Hyderabad's prime attractions is the Salar Jung Museum, the world's largest one-man collection, including unusual collections of jade, marble statues, rare manuscripts, Persian paintings and other objects d'art. A short distance away looms one of the most magnificent fortresses in India, the Golconda Fort. And the Hi-Tec City symbolizes Hyderabad's ascendancy in IT. For more details please Click Here.
